Tips & Tricks

Team Composition

Pair Hu Tao with Xingqiu for consistent Vaporize reactions. Add Zhongli for shield protection and resistance shred. The fourth slot can be flexible with characters like Kazuha, Sucrose, or Yelan.

Combat Strategy

Always use Elemental Skill before attacking. Try to maintain HP below 50% to maximize Hu Tao's passive. Use charged attacks when possible for higher damage output.

Advanced Technique

Learn the "jump cancel" technique after charged attacks to avoid the long recovery animation. This significantly increases your DPS output during Elemental Skill duration.
